=====PRIMARY OBJECTIVE=====
THIS DRILLING PROGRAM IS ISSUED TO DRILL WELL SDGM-1255, PLANNED TO BE DRILLED AS AN S-SHAPE WELL TARGETING PRE-
KHUFF AS PRIMARY TARGET AND KHUFF AS SECONDARY TARGET AND WILL BE COMPLETED WITH 4-1/2" 15K CEMENTED LINER.
THE WELL IS PLANNED AS MK-2 DESIGN FOR FUTURE SIDETRACK REQUIREMENT ACROSS KHUFF OR PRE-KHUFF. IN CASE OF HIGH
PRESSURE FROM JILH, WELL DESIGN WILL BE CONVERTED TO K2PK-S AND SEPARATE SUPPLEMENT WILL BE ISSUED TO DESCRIBE
OPERATIONAL SEQUENCE. A SEPARATE SUPPLEMENT WILL BE ISSUED TO COVER THE COMPLETION DETAILS.
THE WELL IS OUT OF SHALLOW GAS BOUNDARY/AREA. NO INTER FACILITY EMERGENCY RESPONSE PLAN (ERP) IS REQUIRED AS NO
FACILITIES ARE WITHIN 30 PPM RADIUS. HOWEVER, A SITE-SPECIFIC ERP IS ENCLOSED IN THIS DRILLING PROGRAM. A NON
POPULATED ERP WILL BE ISSUED IN A SEPARATE SUPPLEMENT. THE WELL IS LOCATED ON PAD LOCATION WITH WELL
